Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexoraDigital’s digital products, from purchase details to technical support.

What formats are the products delivered in?

Our products are delivered as downloadable ZIP files containing Python scripts, PDFs, videos, or HTML templates, depending on the item.

Do I need advanced skills to use these products?

No, our tools include clear, step-by-step instructions, making them suitable for beginners and intermediate users in IT and development.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund policy. Contact us within 7 days of purchase if the product doesn’t meet your expectations, and we’ll process a refund.

Do you provide updates for the products?

Yes, we provide free updates to ensure our tools stay compatible with the latest technologies. You’ll receive notifications via email.

Are the products compatible with my system?

Our digital products (e.g., Python scripts, PDFs) are designed to work on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Check product descriptions for specific requirements.

How can I get support if I have issues?

Contact us via email (provided in your purchase confirmation) or join our community for assistance. We respond within 24-48 hours.
